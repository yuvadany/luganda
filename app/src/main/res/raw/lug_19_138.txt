 1 Nneebazanga n'omutima gwange gwonna: Mu maaso ga bakatonda naayimbanga okukutendereza. 
2 Nnaasinzizanga eri yeekaalu yo entukuvu, Era nneebazanga erinnya lyo olw'ekisa kyo n'olw'amazima go: Kubanga ogulumizizza ekigambo kyo okusinga erinnya lyo lyonna. 
3 Ku lunaku lwe nnakaabirirako wampitaba, N'oŋŋumya n'amaanyi mu mmeema yange. 
4 Bakabaka bonna ab'ensi balikwebaza, ai Mukama, Kubanga bawulidde ebigambo eby'akamwa ko. 
5 Weewaawo, baliyimba ku makubo ga Mukama; Kubanga ekitiibwa kya Mukama kingi. 
6 Kuba Mukama newakubadde nga ye mukulu, naye n'alowooza abeetoowaza: Naye ab'amalala abamanya ng'ayima wala. 
7 Newakubadde nga ntambulira wakati mu nnaku, olinzuukiza; Oligolola omukono gwo ku busungu bw'abalabe bange, N'omukono gwo ogwa ddyo gulindokola. 
8 Mukama alituukiriza ebigambo ebinfaako: Okusaasira kwo, ai Mukama, kubeerera emirembe gyonna; Toleka mirimu gya mikono gyo ggwe.
