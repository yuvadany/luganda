 1 Ai Katonda, kiki ekyatusuuza ennaku zonna? Kiki ekinyoosa obusungu bwo eri endiga ez'ettale lyo? 
2 Ojjukire ekibiina kyo kye wagula edda, Kye wanunula okuba ekika eky'obusika bwo; N'olusozi Sayuuni, lwe watuulangamu. 
3 Oyimuse ebigere byo ogende mu bifulukwa ebitavaawo, Obubi bwonna omulabe bwe yakola mu watukuvu. 
4 Abalabe bo bawulugumye wakati mu kibiina kyo; Basimbye ebendera zaabwe okuba obubonero. 
5 Bafaananye ng'abantu abayimusa Embazzi okutema ekisaka eky'omu kibira. 
6 Ne kaakano bamenyaamenya emirimu gyawo gyonna emyole N'embazzi n'ennyondo. 
7 Bookezza omuliro awatukuvu wo; Banyoomye ennyumba ey'erinnya lyo ne bagisuula wansi. 
8 Boogedde mu mitima gyabwe nti Tubazikiririze ddala bonna bonna: Bookerezza ddala amakuŋŋaaniro gonna aga Katonda mu nsi. 
9 Tetukyalaba bubonero bwaffe; Tewakyali nnabbi n'omu; So temuli n'omu mu ffe ategeera ebyo gye birituusa okubaawo. 
10 Ai Katonda, omulabe alituusa wa okuvuma? Akukyawa alivvoola erinnya lyo emirembe gyonna? 
11 Kiki ekikuddiriza omukono, omukono gwo ogwa ddyo? Oguggye mu kifuba kyo obazikirize. 
12 Naye Katonda ye Kabaka wange edda n'edda lyonna, Aleeta obulokozi wakati mu nsi. 
13 Wayawula ennyanja n'amaanyi go: Wamenya emitwe gy'ensolo mu mazzi. 
14 Wamenyaamenya emitwe gya lukwata, Wamuwa okuba emmere y'abo abatuula mu ddungu. 
15 Wasalamu ensulo n'omukka: Wakaza emigga eminene. 
16 Emisana gigyo, era n'ekiro kikyo: Wakola omusana n'enjuba. 
17 Wateekawo ensalo zonna ez'ensi: Wakola ekyeya ne ddumbi. 
18 Ojjukire kino ng'abalabe bavumye, ai Mukama, Era ng'eggwanga essirusiru liboggoledde erinnya lyo. 
19 Nkwegayiridde, towaayo emmeeme y'ejjiba lyo eri ensolo: Teweerabira bulamu obw'abaavu bo ennaku zonna. 
20 Ojjukire endagaano: Kubanga ebifo eby'enzikiza eby'ensi bijjudde ennyumba ez'ettima. 
21 Ajoogebwa alemenga okudda ng'akwatiddwa ensonyi, nkwegayiridde: Abaavu n'abanafu batenderezenga erinnya lyo. 
22 Golokoka, ai Katonda, weewolereze ensonga yo, Ojjukire omusirusiru bw'azibya obudde okukuvuma. 
23 Teweerabira ddoboozi lya balabe bo: Oluyoogaano lw'abo abakugolokokerako lulinnya bulijjo.
