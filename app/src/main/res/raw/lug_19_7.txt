1 Ai Mukama, Katonda wange, neesiga ggwe: Ondokole eri abo bonna abangoba, omponye: 
2 Aleme okutaagula emmeeme yange ng'empologoma, Okugitaagulataagula, nga tewali anamponya. 
3 Ai Mukama, Katonda wange, oba nga nakola bwe ntyo; Oba nga obutali butuukirivu buli mu mikono gyange; 
4 Oba nga namuwalana obubi eyali n'emirembe gye ndi; (Nedda, naye nawonya eyali omulabe wange obwereere:) 
5 Ankyawa ayigganye emmeeme yange, agituukeko: Anninnyirire obulamu bwange wansi mu ttaka, Asse ekitiibwa kyange mu nfuufu. (Seera) 
6 Golokoka, ai Mukama, mu busungu bwo, Weesimbe ku bukambwe obw'abalabe bange: Era onzuukukire; walagira omusango. 
7 Era ekkuŋŋaaniro ery'amawanga likwetooloole: Okomewo waggulu okubafuga 
8 Mukama aweereza omusango amawanga: Osale omusango gwange, ai Mukama, olw'obutuukiriw bwange n'amazima gange agali mu nze. 
9 Singa obubi obw'ababi buweddewo, naye abatuukirivu ggwe obanyweze: Kubanga Katonda ow'obutuukirivu ageza emitima n'emmeeme. 
10 Engabo yange eri ne Katonda, Alokola abalina omutima ogw'amazima. 
11 Katonda ye musazi w'emisango omutuukirivu, Era ye Katonda alina obusungu buli lunaku. 
12 Omuntu bw'atakyuka, aliwagala ekitala kye; Aleeze omutego gwe, aguteeseteese. 
13 Era amuteekeddeteekedde ebintu ebitta; Emisaale gye agiteekako omuliro. 
14 Laba, alina olubuto olw'obubi; Weewaawo, eggwako lye lye ttima, era azadde obulimba. 
15 Yakola obunnya, yabusima, Era agudde mu lusalosalo lwe yakola. 
16 Ettima lye liriddira ku mutwe gwe ye, N'ekyejo kye kirituuka ku bwezinge bwe. 
17 Neebazanga Mukama olw'obutuukirivu bwe: Naayimbanga okutendereza erinnya lya Mukama ali waggulu ennyo.
