 1 Nasanyuka bwe baŋŋamba nti Tugende mu nnyumba ya Mukama. 
2 Ebigere byaffe biyimiridde Munda mu miryango gyo, ggwe Yerusaalemi; 
3 Ggwe Yerusaalemi, eyazimbibwa Okuba ekibuga ekigattiddwa awamu: 
4 Ebika gye birinnya, bye bika bya Mukama, Okuba obujulirwa eri Isiraeri, Okwebazanga erinnya lya Mukama. 
5 Kubanga baateeka omwo entebe ez'okusalirako omusango, Entebe ez'ennyumba ya Dawudi. 
6 Musabirenga Yerusaalemi emirembe: Baliraba omukisa abakwagala. 
7 Emirembe gibeere mu bisenge byo, N'omukisa mu mayu go. 
8 Ku lwa baganda bange ne bannange Naayogera kaakano nti Emirembe gibeere mu ggwe. 
9 Olw'ennyumba ya Mukama Katonda waffe Naagezangako okukukola obulungi.
