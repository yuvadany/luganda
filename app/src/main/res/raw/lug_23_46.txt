 1 Beri avuunama, Nebo akutama; ebifaananyi biri ku nsolo ne ku bisolo: ebintu bye mwatambuzatambuzanga bifuuse omugugu, omutwalo eri ensolo ekooye. 
2 Bakutama bavuunama wamu; tebayinza kuwonya mugugu, naye bo bennyini bagenze mu kusibibwa. 
3 Mumpulire ggwe ennyumba ya Yakobo, n'ekitundu kyonna ekifisseewo ku nnyumba ya Isiraeri, be nnaweekanga nze okuva mu lubuto, be nnasitulanga okuva munda: 
4 n'okutuusa ku bukadde nze nzuuyo: n'okutuusa ku nvi naabasitulanga: nze nakola era nze naaweekanga: weewaawo, naasitulanga era naawonyanga. 
5 Ani gwe mulinfaananya era gwe mulinnenkanya era gwe mulingererako tufaanane? 
6 Abaggya ezaabu nnyingi mu nsawo ne bapima effeeza mu minzaani, bagulirira omuweesi wa zaabu n'agifuula katonda; bavuunamira, weewaawo, basinza. 
7 Bamukongojja ku kibegabega, bamusitula ne bamusimba mu kifo kye n'ayimirira; mu kifo kye talisegukawo: weewaawo, walibaawo alimukaabirira, naye tayinza kwanukula newakubadde okumuwonya mu nnaku ze. 
8 Mujjukire ekyo, mwerage obusajja: mukijjukire nate, mmwe aboonoonyi. 
9 Mujjukire ebigambo ebyasooka eby'edda: kubanga nze Katonda so tewali mulala; nze Katonda so tewali anfaanana; 
10 alanga enkomerero okuva ku lubereberye, n'ebigambo ebitannakolebwa okuva ku biro eby'edda; ayogera nti Okuteesa kwange kulinywera era ndikola bye njagala byonna: 
11 ayita ennyonyi ey'amaddu akuva ebuvanjuba, omusajja ow'okuteesa kwange okuva mu nsi ey'ewala; weewaawo, nayogera, n'okutuukiriza ndikituukiriza; nateesa, n'okukola ndikikola. 
12 Mumpulirize, mmwe abalina emitima emikakanyavu, abali ewala n'obutuukirivu: 
13 nsembeza obutuukirivu bwange, tebuliba wala n'obulokozi bwange tebulirwawo era nditeeka obulokozi mu Sayuuni ku lwa Isiraeri ekitiibwa kyange.
